Every time I visit, I ride a bike along the river from dawn to dusk, enjoying the misty mornings, flowing water, temples, tea houses, and quiet everyday life. The calm rhythm of the river, mixed with birdsong, wind, and simple meals, helps me slow down and relax. This kind of travel feels simple but deeply meaningful to me.
